Our Story

Where Passion Meets Premium Baking

Founded in the heart of Iseyin, Oyo State, Ewa's Cakes and Pastries began with one simple mission: to make every celebration unforgettable through artistry, flavour and love. "Ewa" means beauty — and beauty is baked into everything we make.

From towering wedding cakes to trays of golden small chops, every order is crafted with premium ingredients, strict hygiene standards and a designer's eye for detail.

Meet The Founder

Ms Olamide

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 5 years of professional baking and entrepreneurship, Ms Olamide built Ewa's Cakes and Pastries from a home kitchen into one of Iseyin's most loved cake studios. Her obsession with detail, flavour and customer happiness shapes every creation that leaves our kitchen.

She leads a passionate team of bakers and decorators committed to turning your celebrations into beautiful, edible memories.

"Every cake we bake carries a piece of our heart. Your joy is the secret ingredient."
